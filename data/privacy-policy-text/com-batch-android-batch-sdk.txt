Data shared: data collected and communicated with third parties.Security practices and data handling.Information provided by developers is shown in your app listing on the PlayStore, allowing final users to know the kind of data your app collects before installing your app. ‚ö†Ô∏è Important note: Developers will be required to fill in the Data Safety form in the Play Console before July 20th, 2022.Completing the Data Safety formImportant noteBefore filling in the questionnaire, please take some time to:üìö Read the documentation: Make sure you read Batch documentation on the data you are collecting by default via the SDK.‚öôÔ∏è Check your integration: Developers can disable part of the data collection or collect additional data if needed.
