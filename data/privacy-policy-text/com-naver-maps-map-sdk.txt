NAVER Cloud Platform privacy policy
NAVER Cloud Corporation (hereafter referred to as the "company") complies with personal information
protection regulations stipulated in relevant laws and regulations that should be observed by information and
communication service providers, such as the Personal Information Protection Act, Act on Promotion of
Information and Communication Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc., Protection of
Communications Secrets Act, and Telecommunications Business Act. The company also does its best to protect
the rights and interests of customers by establishing the privacy policy based on the relevant laws and
regulations. This privacy policy shall apply to the services provided by the company and includes following
materials.

Article 2 (Personal information collection methods)
① The company shall collect the personal information using the following methods.
1. Collection from the website, document, fax, phone, counseling bulletin board, offline collection (through
events or seminars)
2. Collection from partner companies
3. Collection using generated information collection tools
Chapter 2. Purpose of collecting and using the personal information
Article 3 (Fulfilling the contract concerning service provision and fee payment for service provision)
① The company shall collect the personal information for service-related purposes such as providing content and
specific customized service, delivering goods or invoices, identity authentication, purchase and payment, and
collection of fees.
Article 4 (Member management)
① The Company collects personal information for member management purposes to verify user identities for using
member services, to confirm individual identification, to prevent illegal use and unauthorized use by inadequate
members, to confirm account registrations, to prevent duplicate registrations, to validate identities of legal
representatives, to archive records for dispute resolution, to process civil complaints, to provide notices, and to
confirm member's decision to withdraw membership.
Article 5 (Utilization for new service development and marketingadvertising)
The company shall collect the personal information for the purposes related to new service development and
marketing activities, such as developing a new service and providing a customized service, providing services and
advertising according to statistical characteristics, checking the effectiveness of service, providing event and
advertising information and a participation opportunity, understanding the access frequency, and compiling
statistics on members’ service use.
