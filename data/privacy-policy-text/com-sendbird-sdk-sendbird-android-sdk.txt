Thank you for using SendBird!
Last Updated: June 22, 2022SendBird, Inc. and our wholly owned subsidiary SendBird Korea, Inc. (“SendBird”) is committed to respecting the privacy rights of its customers, visitors and other users of its services. We know that you care about how your Personal Information is used and shared, and therefore we take your privacy seriously. You have control over what information you share and whom you share it with. We do not share your Personal Information with people or services without your permission. We will work hard to be a responsible steward of the information we hold. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy practices (“Privacy Notice”).
By using the services provided by SendBird, any other related services, or using the SendBird Website at www.SendBird.com (collectively, the “Services”), you accept the practices outlined in this Privacy Notice.
If you do not accept any of the provisions of this Privacy Notice, please do not use the Services.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice, you may contact us at privacy@sendbird.com or by writing us at: SendBird (ATTN: Privacy), 400 1st Ave. San Mateo, CA 94401, United States.
If you have any questions or security reports, please contact us at security@sendbird.com

Personal Information
SendBird collects information that you provide us. For example, when you express an interest in obtaining additional information about the Services or registering to use the Services, SendBird may ask you to provide certain contact information such as name, organization name, address, email address, and phone number (collectively, “Contact Information”). When registering for the Services, you may be required to provide additional information that may include a credit card number or other banking or payment information (“Billing Information”).
In connection with your use of the Services, SendBird may collect information that is created or provided by you for such purposes (collectively, “Service Information”). This may include but is not limited to: (a) personal data, such as information you provide about other people such as contact information, phone number, email address, first and last names, (b) message usage data, such as messaging history, transaction dates, read receipts, IP address and other messaging metadata and (c) call usage data, such as call history, call recipient information, call transaction dates, IP address and other call metadata. Contact Information, Billing Information and Service Information may include information that is “Personal Information,” meaning data which directly, or used in conjunction with other provided information, enables the identification of a specific individual.
Site Information
As you navigate the SendBird Website, SendBird may also collect information through the use of commonly-used information-gathering tools, such as cookies and Web beacons (“Site Information”). Site Information includes standard information from your Web browser (such as browser type and browser language), your Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, and the actions you take on the Site (such as the Web pages viewed and the links clicked). Contact Information, Billing Information, Service Information and Site Information may be collectively referred to as the “Data.”
California Civil Code
Under California Civil Code Sections 1798.83-1798.84, California residents are entitled to ask us for a notice identifying the categories of Personal Information which we share with our affiliates and/or third parties for marketing purposes, and providing contact information for such affiliates and/or third parties. If you are a California resident and would like a copy of this notice, please submit a written request to privacy@sendbird.com
